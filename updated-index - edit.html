About Us

KRIA EDIFY is working under KRIA Edify Private Limited. It is incorporated under Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India based at Calicut, Kerala and its regional office located in Cochin, Kerala. Our focus is to provide an open learning platform, where each individual can explore and unleash their potential.

Our Mission - Creating employment opportunities for the youth and marginalized communities . Enhancing and enabling the capabilities of individuals to live a successful life by way of holistic skill development , Build a collaborative network, for sustainable livelihood.

Vision - We believe passionately in the power of skilling; where we aim to change lives and attitudes, create economic opportunities, build and empower communities that are sustainable in order to address the challenges of our Nation.
